Silver Membership

Silver membership is ideal for patients who see their doctor only a few times a year, but always have access to them.

Amount:Rs.850

Benefits

  • Special pricing on lab work

  • Special pricing on in-office tests/labs

  • Special pricing for in-office procedures

  • Telehealth

  • Same or Next Day Appointments

Gold Membership

The Gold Membership is ideal for people that are more health conscientious or have chronic medical conditions which requires monitoring on a frequent basis.

Amount:Rs.1200

Benefits

  • Standard lab work

  • EKG

  • Rapid Strep test

  • Extended appointment times

Platinum Membership

For patients who want to have all the facilites of the hospital

Amount:Rs.1500

Benefits

  • ALL lab work including: Rapid Strep and Influenza Testing

  • ALL in-office testing: EKG, PFT, Hearing and Vascular Screening

  • ALL in-office procedures

  • X-rays
